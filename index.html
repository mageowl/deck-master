Tutorial

Hello Player.
Soon you'll be battling monsters and striving against death, but I would suggest reading this first.

This is your player:


To move, you drag your player to a card that is 1 space away from you (Inculding diagonals.) You can also use the arrow keys or the left joy stick on a controller (A list of supported controllers is at the bottom.)

If the first number in the top-left goes down to 0, you die. This is your health. It cannot go above the second number.

In the real game, there'll be a 3x3 grid of diffent cards to move to, containing cards of these types:


This is a coin card (yellow border). It gives you coins equal to the top-left number (value) that you can then spend on items or keep to raise your score.

To access the item store, click your coins in the bottom-left corner. You can also press the down arrow or the Y button on a controller.


This is a monster (dark green border). When you move to it, you lose health equal to its value.

At the end of the game, your score is increased by the number and values of any monsters you killed.


This is an effect (gray border). Diffrent effect cards do diffrent things, but this one heals you the number of points equal to its value.


This is a monster-effect (light green border). It's a mix of the previous two types, so when you move to it, you lose health equal to its value, and, it acts as an effect card (Usually it is a negitive effect).
This card give all Willow Wisps (a type of monster) +1 value (indecated by the .)


This card is an item. They do diffrent things, and they can be bought from the store for coins by clicking on them (Cost is in the bottom.) You can also press arrow keys/left joy stick while the store is open to select a card, and then enter/the A button to buy a card.

You finished this tutorial! Go play the game!



A Word on Controllers

To use a controller, connect your computer to the controller and press a button. Then in then main menu you can click "Use Controller."

The button text should then change to the name of your contoller. If it doesn't, please make sure you followed the directions and the controller is supported.

Supported Contollers

  • Nindendo Joy-Cons
  • Xbox 360/One Controller
